Privacy Policy for MailArmor

Last updated: June 6, 2026

MailArmor is a Chrome extension that detects phishing emails in Gmail.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Email content analyzed by MailArmor is processed locally on your device and immediately discarded after scanning. We never store your emails.

Scan Statistics

Basic usage statistics such as number of scans and threats detected are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device.

Payments

Pro subscriptions are processed by Gumroad. Please refer to Gumroad's privacy policy at gumroad.com/privacy for details on how payment data is handled.
